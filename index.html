About Me

I moved with my family to Tennesee on May 10, 2003, when I was still in elementary school. My family and I have been living here ever since then, living life and trying to make ends meet. It was somewhat hard to adjust to the different culture, atmosphere, and the people. It was a struggle to interact as well as fit in with people who come from different backgrounds.
